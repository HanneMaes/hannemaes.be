Finding an A.I.'s artistic vision

Finding the perfect intersection between a human and an A.I. artist.
Just like the pop art movement in the 50s I try to experiment with new technologies.
In my case I use A.I. techniques to create unique imagery.

The Experiment

In this visual experiment I compared myself to an A.I. that is given various degrees of artistic freedom and abstraction.

The work on the bottom left is how I created it.
Going along the x-axis I give the A.I. more creative freedom. Along the y-axis I abstract the input the A.I. is given.
This culminates in the upper right work where the A.I. is given full creative control over an abstracted source resulting in an artistic vision created by the A.I. artist itself.

The abstraction number represents the resolution of the applied abstraction.
The keyframes number represents the amount of keyframes I fed the A.I. to base his output on. When the keyframes are negative, I used that amount of "incorrect" keyframe to confuse the A.I. and let it express itself in an unintended way, forcing the A.I. to be "creative".

The Chosen One

The right panel represents the ideal intersection between a human and an A.I. artist.
This is where Async.art's programmable technology comes into play, upon owning this piece you can decide what panel is displayed and thus express your vision on the matter.
