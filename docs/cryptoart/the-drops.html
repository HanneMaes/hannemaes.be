About The Drops Project



@Nebenzu created The Drops character to functions as a canvas for creatives to create their very own version in their own style.
It was born to connect artists and collectors in an intimate space.

More about The Drop at www.thedrops.xyz

My Drops

For this drop @Nebenzu teamed up with veefriends for their PLP Project.
Each artist created 2 works of art, 1 to sell and 1 to give away for free to veefriends holders.

The portrait view is the minted version.
The full body is unlockable content only accessible to the owner of the piece.
