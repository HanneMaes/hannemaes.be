12 Years

A unique artwork, every hour, for 12 years.

3 structures, each rotating at a different speed.
The left one rotates in 7 days, the middle one in 30 days and the right one takes 365 days complete its cycle
It takes 12 years for the whole work to complete all the different configurations and arrive back at its origin state.

When you look at the piece, you know it will take 12 years (leap years included) to rotate to that exact same configuration.
It will take 12 years for you to see that same configuration ever again.

Timing

- Structure 1 completes its rotation in 7 days with a new state every hour.
- Structure 2 completes its rotation in 30 days with a new state every 4 hours.
- Structure 3 completes its rotation in 365.25 days with a new state 6 hours.
This makes it that the full cycle completes in 12 years.
